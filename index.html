Trent Wintermeier is a Rhetoric PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin. His research interests include: sound, noise, digital rhetorics, and the digital humanities. Currently, he is working on making audiovisual artifacts more accessible and discoverable as a research assistant for AVAnnotate and a facilitator for the Undergraduate Research Apprenticeship Program.

His full cv is available on UT Austin's Rhetoric and Writing Graduate Students landing page.

He can be contacted via email at trentwintermeier@utexas.edu.

Listening Across the Shortwave was interdisciplinary Build Day held by the Digital Writing and Research Lab during November 2023. Participants worked together to construct DIY radio receivers, based on the open-source instructions from international feminist art group Shortwave Collective. Not only was this the DWRL's first build day of the year, but Listening Across the Shortwave was the first in-person workshop using the Shortwave Collective instructions in the United States.
